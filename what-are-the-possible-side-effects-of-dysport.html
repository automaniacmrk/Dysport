What are the possible side effects of Dysport?

While infrequent, mild temporary bruising is the most common side effect. Dysport may cause temporary weakness in nearby muscles and migrates occasionally. Rarely, Dysport can cause a loss of vision or an asymmetry in facial expressions. The injections used will determine the risk. Before you have treatment, your doctor will talk to you about this. Because the effects of Dysport are completely reversible, any side effects are temporary, typically lasting only a few weeks. For best results, it is crucial to find an experienced injector who has received advanced training to reduce the chance of side effects.
